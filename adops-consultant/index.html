Campaign performance depends on operations being accurate before optimization begins.

Wrong macros, inconsistent naming, incomplete QA and unclear ownership can waste budget while the dashboards still appear normal.

I review how campaigns are created, checked, launched, monitored and reported, including the handoffs between commercial, media buying, tracking and technical teams.

The objective is a practical AdOps system that reduces preventable errors, makes troubleshooting faster and creates reliable inputs for optimization.
