An affiliate program is a commercial and operational product offered to partners.

Partners need a credible offer, transparent conditions, functioning tracking, timely communication and evidence that traffic can be monetized fairly.

I help define the proposition, payout logic, attribution, validation rules, reporting and internal ownership required before recruitment begins.

For existing programs, the review can focus on partner activation, dormant relationships, traffic quality, communication and the gap between promised and actual operating conditions.

Who this is for

For advertisers building a program or trying to make an existing one productive.

The consulting can support digital products, subscriptions, lead-generation businesses, ecommerce companies and vertical-specific advertisers with a suitable partner opportunity.

Designed from both network and traffic perspectives.

A strong program must make sense to the advertiser and to the partner expected to send traffic. I use both perspectives when reviewing conditions and operations.

Frequently asked questions

Can you recruit publishers directly?

Recruitment support can include strategy, materials and outreach process. Direct execution depends on scope and market.

Can you review our payout model?

Yes, using available unit economics, validation, quality and partner competitiveness.

Do we need a network or in-house platform?

That depends on control, resources, partner base and technical requirements. The engagement can compare options.

Can you help hire an affiliate manager?

Yes. I can help define the role, score candidates and create the operating framework they will inherit.
