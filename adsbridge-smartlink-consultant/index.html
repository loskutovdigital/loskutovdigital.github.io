A SmartLink should be a transparent decision system, not a black box full of overlapping rules.

Good routing uses available signals to improve relevance and economics while remaining testable, maintainable and measurable.

I help define campaign and rotator structure, segmentation conditions, fallback logic, token flow and postback requirements inside AdsBridge.

The setup can support affiliate teams, traffic owners and solo buyers who need one controlled routing layer across offers, GEOs, devices or partner destinations.

Who this is for

For teams that have outgrown a basic offer rotation.

The service is useful when traffic must be split by meaningful characteristics, several systems exchange IDs, or an existing SmartLink has become difficult to audit and maintain.

Routing decisions are tied to monetization and reporting.

I approach AdsBridge as part of the campaign operating model, not an isolated technical tool. The rules must support offer fit, data quality and practical optimization.

06 /

Frequently asked questions

Can you build a SmartLink from scratch?

Yes. The project can include structure, rules, tokens, postbacks, testing and documentation.

Can you repair an existing SmartLink?

Yes. I can audit overlapping conditions, broken paths, reporting limitations and identifier flow.

Do you support cross-tracking?

Yes, when the participating platforms can pass and return stable identifiers safely.

Can the setup include payout values?

Yes. Payout and revenue values can be included where the partner and tracking systems support them.
