A SmartLink is ready to scale only when traffic classification, routing, measurement and failure handling are all testable.

One broken identifier or undefined fallback can invalidate the performance data produced by the rest of the system.

This checklist is designed for affiliates, media buyers, networks and technical operators. It is provider-neutral and can be applied to a network SmartLink, an AdsBridge implementation or a custom routing layer.

Mark each item as passed, failed or not applicable. A failed item should have an owner, corrective action and retest date.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use this checklist for AdsBridge?

Yes. The checks apply to AdsBridge and other tracking or SmartLink systems, although interface-specific steps will differ.

Do all forty checks have to pass?

Items may be not applicable, but every failure that can affect eligibility, attribution, compliance or revenue should be resolved before scaling.

How often should the audit be repeated?

Repeat after major routing, payout, offer, tracking or traffic-source changes, and periodically for high-volume systems.

Can you perform the audit independently?

Yes. The review can produce a risk register, corrected parameter map and prioritized implementation plan.
