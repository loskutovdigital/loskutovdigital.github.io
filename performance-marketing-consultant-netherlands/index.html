Performance marketing is a management system, not just a collection of advertising channels.

The useful question is not which platform generated the cheapest conversion. It is which acquisition path created the strongest business outcome after quality, delay and downstream value are considered.

I help define the measurement model, align KPIs with the actual commercial objective and identify where acquisition data becomes unreliable or misleading.

From campaign structure to landing-page behaviour and reporting, the goal is to create a practical cycle of measurement, learning, prioritization and controlled experimentation.
