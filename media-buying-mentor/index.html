Mentoring should make you more independent, not dependent on another person’s daily approval.

The objective is to improve how you structure tests, read data, investigate problems and decide whether to stop, iterate or scale.

We can review one active campaign, a tracking setup, an offer-selection problem or the wider way you organize buying and reporting.

Sessions are practical and based on your real accounts, exports and constraints. The output is a short list of actions and the reasoning behind them.

Who this is for

For solo media buyers who already have something concrete to review.

This is not a zero-to-hero course. It is best for someone who has campaigns, a tracker, an offer, data or a specific technical and commercial problem.

Direct, independent and grounded in your setup.

There is no requirement to use a specific network, traffic source or tool. The focus is on improving your operating decisions with the systems you actually have.

06 /

Frequently asked questions

Can you teach a complete beginner?

A focused starter consultation is possible, but the service is most effective when there is a real setup or campaign to review.

Can we review AdsBridge?

Yes, including SmartLinks, tokens, postbacks and reporting structure.

Will you share winning campaigns?

No. The value is in decision frameworks and analysis, not copying confidential campaigns.

How long is a session?

The format depends on scope. A focused review can often be handled in one session with a written follow-up.
