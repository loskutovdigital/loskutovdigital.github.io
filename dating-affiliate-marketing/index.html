Dating performance depends on the fit between audience, promise, flow and commercial model.

A campaign can generate registrations and still fail because user intent, GEO economics, payment behaviour or downstream quality do not match the offer.

I review the journey from source and creative through landing page, registration, lead qualification and paid action, using the data available to identify where value is lost.

The work can support CPL, CPA, revshare and hybrid models, with attention to traffic quality, approval logic, partner expectations and tracking accuracy.
